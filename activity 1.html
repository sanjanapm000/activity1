Analog vs. Digital — what's the difference?

ANALOG

Measures things that change smoothly — like temperature or speed — using dials, needles or moving parts. Think wall clocks, thermometers and gauges.

DIGITAL

Turns everything into exact numbers and shows it on an electronic screen. Think laptops, phones and calculators.
